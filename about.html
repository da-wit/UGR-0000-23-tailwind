Visited places

Lalibela

Lalibela, an ancient religious site, unveiled its mystical charm through a cluster of rock-hewn churches, intricately carved from solid stone. As I wandered through the underground passages and observed the breathtaking craftsmanship of these churches, I was enveloped in a sense of wonder and reverence for the ingenuity of the artisans who sculpted these architectural marvels centuries ago. The spiritual ambiance and historical significance of Lalibela's churches left an indelible impression on my understanding of Ethiopia's religious heritage. More...

Gonder

Gonder beckoned with its regal magnificence, notably showcased in the Royal Enclosure. The complex, adorned with towering castles, imposing battlements, and grand palaces, resonated with the splendor of Ethiopia's royal history. Exploring the intricately designed structures within the enclosure, I was transported to an era of opulence and regal sophistication. The architectural intricacies and the stories enshrined within the walls of Gonder's historical sites deepened my appreciation for the country's royal legacy and its significance in Ethiopian history.
